01 — Executive Summary
Transforming Policy Management for Clicks
From manual processes to intelligent, automated governance

Clicks operates in a highly regulated retail and healthcare environment where policy compliance is not optional — it is a business imperative. From pharmaceutical dispensing protocols to employment practices, every policy must be current, acknowledged, and auditable.

PolicyIQ is an enterprise-grade policy governance platform built on Microsoft 365 and SharePoint Online. It delivers end-to-end policy lifecycle management — from authoring and approval through distribution, acknowledgement, quiz-based comprehension testing, and compliance analytics — all within the Microsoft ecosystem Clicks already uses.

With AI-powered capabilities including an intelligent policy assistant, automated quiz generation, and bulk document classification, PolicyIQ eliminates manual overhead while strengthening compliance posture across the entire organisation.

02 — The Challenge
Why Policy Management Matters for Clicks
Retail, pharmacy, and healthcare compliance across a national footprint

Regulatory Exposure

Pharmaceutical, health & safety, and employment regulations demand provable compliance. Manual tracking creates audit gaps that carry legal and financial risk.

Manual Overhead

Email-based distribution, spreadsheet tracking, and paper-based acknowledgements consume countless hours from HR, compliance, and line managers.

Distributed Workforce

Hundreds of store locations, pharmacies, and support offices mean policy changes must reach every employee — reliably, verifiably, on time.

Version Control Chaos

Multiple versions of policies across shared drives, intranets, and email attachments create confusion about which version is current and authoritative.

"The cost of non-compliance is always higher than the cost of compliance. PolicyIQ ensures Clicks has a single source of truth for every policy, every acknowledgement, and every audit trail — in real time."

PolicyIQ includes a conversational AI assistant that understands your organisation's policies. Employees can ask natural-language questions and get instant, accurate answers with citations back to source policies — no more searching through documents or waiting for HR to respond.

07 — Business Scenarios
PolicyIQ in Action at Clicks
Real-world scenarios demonstrating measurable value

1 New Store Opening — Onboarding 40 Employees

  1. Admin creates a Policy Pack containing all mandatory policies (Code of Conduct, H&S, Data Privacy, Pharmacy Protocols)
  2. Pack is assigned to all new hires via a Distribution Campaign targeting the new store's security group
  3. Employees receive branded email notifications with direct links to each policy
  4. Each policy requires acknowledgement — some with comprehension quizzes (auto-generated by AI)
  5. Manager Dashboard shows real-time compliance progress — 100% within 5 days
  6. Complete audit trail stored for regulatory inspection

2 Regulatory Update — Pharmacy Dispensing Protocol Change

  1. Author opens existing policy, creates a new revision (original stays published during editing)
  2. Updated content goes through multi-level approval (Pharmacy Manager → Compliance Officer → Legal)
  3. On publish, PolicyIQ auto-distributes to all pharmacy staff across every store
  4. Automated reminders escalate: Day 1 friendly → Day 3 manager CC → Day 5 overdue alert
  5. Executive Dashboard shows national compliance rate climbing in real time
  6. Non-compliant employees flagged for manager follow-up — zero manual tracking

3 Annual Compliance Audit

  1. Auditor requests proof of policy acknowledgement for the past 12 months
  2. Compliance Officer opens Analytics Dashboard → generates reports in seconds
  3. Per-policy acknowledgement rates, individual employee histories, and complete audit trails exported
  4. Quiz scores prove comprehension, not just "checkbox compliance"
  5. Immutable, timestamped records with digital signatures — audit passes with full evidence
