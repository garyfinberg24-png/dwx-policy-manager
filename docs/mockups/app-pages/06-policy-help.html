Frequently Asked Questions
How do I acknowledge a policy?
Compliance
▼
Navigate to My Policies and find the policy marked as "Pending". Click "Read Policy" to open the document. You must scroll through the entire document and wait for the reading timer to complete. If a quiz is required, you'll need to pass it with at least 80%. Finally, click "I Acknowledge" to complete the process. Your acknowledgement is digitally signed and timestamped.
