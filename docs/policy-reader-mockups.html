1. Purpose

This policy establishes the framework for corporate governance across the organisation, ensuring accountability, transparency, and compliance with regulatory requirements.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all directors, officers, and employees of the organisation and its subsidiaries. External contractors and consultants are expected to comply with relevant sections during their engagement.

3. Governance Framework

3.1 Board Responsibilities

The Board of Directors is responsible for setting the strategic direction of the organisation, overseeing management, and ensuring fiduciary duties are fulfilled.

3.2 Executive Accountability

Executive leadership is accountable for implementing board-approved strategies, maintaining internal controls, and reporting to the board on performance.

4. Compliance Requirements

All activities must comply with applicable laws, regulations, and industry standards. Non-compliance must be reported immediately through the established escalation channels.

  • All employees must ensure that documents are placed in official filing systems
  • Employees must not remove documents from official files
  • Records must be complete, up-to-date, and capable of providing organisational accountability

5. Review and Amendment

This policy will be reviewed annually by the Governance Committee and amended as necessary to reflect changes in legislation or organisational structure.

Reading time: ~5 min | 35% complete

Design Rationale

  • Stepper replaced with reading progress bar — visual progress without taking vertical space
  • Metadata compressed into single strip — policy name, badges, version all in one line
  • Document centered at 800px max-width — optimal reading line length (65-75 characters)
  • No sidebar, no linked records, no document viewer chrome — just the content
  • Sticky bottom action bar — "I Have Read" button always visible without scrolling back up
  • Reading time + completion % — gives user context without clutter
  • ~85% of screen is document content (vs ~40% in current layout)
Variation B: Split Panel with Collapsible Sidebar Document takes 75% width. Right sidebar has metadata, key points, linked docs — collapsible.

Design Rationale

  • 100% document width — no sidebar, no split, maximum reading space
  • Floating action card — always visible in bottom-right, doesn't take layout space
  • Step progress as 4 mini bars — visual and compact
  • "More Info" expandable — metadata available on demand, not always shown
  • Reading progress bar at top — user knows how far they've scrolled
  • ~90% of screen is document content (vs ~40% in current layout)
  • Inspiration: Medium.com / Notion reading experience

Recommendation: Variation C (Floating Card) gives the cleanest reading experience with 90% document space. Variation B (Split Panel) is the safest choice if stakeholders want metadata always visible. Variation A is ideal for a "focus mode" toggle.
