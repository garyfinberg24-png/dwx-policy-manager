Quality Management System

First Digital — ISO 9001:2015 Certified

Our Quality Management System (QMS) provides the framework for consistently delivering products and services that meet client expectations and regulatory requirements.

Quality Policy: First Digital is committed to delivering digital workplace solutions that exceed client expectations through continuous improvement, innovation, and a culture of quality in everything we do.

PDCA Cycle — Continuous Improvement

Plan

  • Define objectives and KPIs
  • Identify risks and opportunities
  • Allocate resources and responsibilities

Do

  • Execute planned activities
  • Follow documented procedures
  • Collect data and evidence

Check

  • Monitor and measure results
  • Internal audits and management reviews
  • Analyse data for trends

Act

  • Implement corrective actions
  • Update processes and procedures
  • Share lessons learned
2 / 4

Quality Objectives 2026

  • Client satisfaction score: maintain 4.5/5.0 or above
  • Project delivery on-time rate: achieve 92% (up from 88%)
  • Defect escape rate: reduce by 20% year-on-year
  • Internal audit non-conformities: zero critical findings
  • Employee quality training completion: 100% within 30 days
  • Supplier quality incidents: fewer than 3 per quarter

Measurement

Quality metrics are tracked in real-time through the DWx Analytics Dashboard and reviewed monthly by the Quality Steering Committee.

Your Role in Quality

Quality is everyone's responsibility

  • Follow documented procedures and work instructions for your role
  • Report non-conformities and near-misses through the QMS portal
  • Participate in quality improvement initiatives and retrospectives
  • Complete quality-related training on time
  • Suggest process improvements — every idea is valued
