1. Purpose

To protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of organisational information assets by defining security controls and responsibilities.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party service providers who access the organisation's information systems and data.

3. Data Classification

All information must be classified according to the following levels:

  • Public — Information intended for public access
  • Internal — General business information for internal use
  • Confidential — Sensitive business information with restricted access
  • Restricted — Highly sensitive information requiring maximum protection

4. Access Control

Access to information systems must follow the principle of least privilege. Multi-factor authentication is required for all privileged access and remote connections.

5. Incident Response

Security incidents must be reported within 1 hour of discovery to the IT Security team. The incident response plan must be followed for containment, eradication, and recovery.

6. Acceptable Use

Organisational IT resources must be used for legitimate business purposes. Personal use is permitted within reasonable limits as defined in the Acceptable Use Guidelines.
