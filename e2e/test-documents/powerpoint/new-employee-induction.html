Your 90-Day Journey

Days 1-30: Learn

  • Complete all mandatory training
  • Understand team goals and processes
  • Build relationships with key stakeholders
  • Month 1 check-in with manager

Days 31-60: Contribute

  • Take ownership of initial projects
  • Provide fresh-eyes feedback
  • Deepen technical/domain knowledge
  • Mid-probation review with manager

Days 61-90: Own

  • Deliver meaningful outcomes independently
  • Mentor newer joiners if applicable
  • Set goals for the next quarter
  • Final probation review — confirmation of appointment
