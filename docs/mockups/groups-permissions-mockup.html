Workflow Groups define who can review and approve policies. These groups are referenced by approval workflow templates — when a policy is submitted for review, members of the assigned reviewer/approver group are notified.
🔍
▶ 👥
PM_PolicyReviewers
Users who review policy drafts before approval
REVIEWERS 5 members
▶ 👥
PM_PolicyApprovers
Users who give final approval to publish policies
APPROVERS 3 members
💡 Tip: You can create custom workflow groups for department-specific reviews. For example, PM_FinanceReviewers for finance policies, PM_HRApprovers for HR policies. Reference these groups in your Approval Workflow templates.
